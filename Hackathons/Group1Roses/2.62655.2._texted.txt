Origin:
Introduced in United States by Jackson & Perkins (Wholesale) as 'Love's Kiss'. 

Class:
Hybrid Tea.  

Bloom:
Bright pink petal is outlined in a deep shade of roseé, accentuating its size and texture..  Strong, damask, pepper, sweet fragrance.  35 petals.  Average diameter 5".  Large, full (26-40 petals), classic hybrid tea bloom form.  Blooms in flushes throughout the season.  Pointed, ovoid buds.  

Habit:
Medium, compact.  Dark green foliage.  Height: 4' to 5' (120 to 150cm).  Width: 4' (120cm). 

Growing:
USDA zone 5b through 9a.  Can be used for container rose, cut flower or garden.  Prefers full sun.
